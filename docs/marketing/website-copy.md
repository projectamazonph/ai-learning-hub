## About page

### Headline

AI education should make people more capable, not more dependent on a tool.

### Body

AI Ready PH was created by Ryan Roland Dabao for people who need to use AI in real work without losing judgment, evidence, or control.

The learning system comes from a simple belief: confidence should follow practice. Learners work with realistic synthetic tasks, see what quality requires, revise weak output, and keep proof of what they can do.

## FAQ

### Do I need AI experience?

No. The starter course assumes you are new. It explains the important concepts in plain language and gives you a first guided task.

### Do I need a paid AI account?

No for the free starter and demo labs. Paid plans include a defined live-practice allowance inside the platform.

### Will this teach one specific AI tool?

The core teaches transferable skills: task framing, context, verification, quality, workflow design, safety, and business value. Tool walkthroughs appear as reviewed supplements when useful.

### Can I use real client data in the labs?

Required scenarios use synthetic data. Do not upload client secrets, credentials, sensitive personal data, or contract-restricted information. The platform provides safer practice alternatives.

### Does the certificate guarantee a job or income?

No. The certificate verifies that you met defined learning and evidence requirements. It does not guarantee employment, clients, income, or provider endorsement.

### What makes the certificate credible?

Certificates require competency evidence and a capstone. Public verification shows the course, issue date, status, and learner-approved Skills Passport evidence.

### Can business managers read employee prompts and files?

Private personal practice stays private by default. Workspace managers receive the progress and competency information defined by the workspace policy. Workspace-owned assignments state visibility before work begins.

### What happens when AI tools change?

Lessons and simulations carry sources, versions, reviewed dates, volatility levels, and owners. Volatile content enters a scheduled review queue instead of quietly becoming ancient history.

### Can the labs perform real automations?

Not in the MVP. Workflow and agent labs simulate tools, permissions, failures, approvals, and rollback using synthetic systems. Real connectors require additional security and confirmation controls.

### Can I learn on a phone?

Yes. Core lessons and most labs are designed for mobile. Complex workflow diagrams use an ordered-step editor on small screens.
