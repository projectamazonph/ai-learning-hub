## Beta FAQ response

**Is this free?**  
The founding beta access and obligations are stated on the application and acceptance page. No charge can occur without a separate checkout showing the exact product, amount, access, and refund terms.

**Do I need ChatGPT Plus or another paid account?**  
No. Demo activities do not require a provider account. Any included live practice is handled inside the platform and shows its credit use.

**Can I use client work?**  
Use the synthetic scenarios provided. Do not paste client secrets or sensitive personal data.

**Will this get me hired?**  
The beta helps you build and explain practical evidence. It does not guarantee employment, clients, or income.

**Can I join using only a phone?**  
Yes for the required beta path. We specifically need mobile feedback.
