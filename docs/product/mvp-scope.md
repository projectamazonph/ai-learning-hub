# MVP Scope

## Scope rule

The MVP proves that beginners can produce, improve, and retain evidence of useful AI-assisted work. Features that do not strengthen that loop are deferred.

## Must ship

### Acquisition and onboarding

- Outcome-led public site.
- Separate VA, freelancer, and business-owner pages.
- Registration and secure account lifecycle.
- AI readiness diagnostic and explainable path recommendation.
- Free AI Ready in 90 Minutes course.

### Learning

- Practical AI Foundations.
- AI-Powered Virtual Assistant path.
- Course, module, lesson, resource, quiz, prerequisite, and progress engine.
- Eleven competencies and evidence timeline.
- Content source, version, volatility, and review metadata.

### Practice

- Generic lab engine.
- Demo, practice, and assessment modes.
- Prompt Workbench.
- Brief-to-Deliverable Studio.
- Research and Verification Lab.
- Output QA Lab.
- Data Safety Clinic.
- Save, resume, submit, evaluate, revise, and complete lifecycle.
- AI gateway, credits, safety checks, usage, cost, and version capture.

### Proof

- Portfolio Builder sufficient for capstone evidence.
- Skills Passport with private and shareable views.
- Foundation and VA capstones.
- Human moderation.
- Verified PDF certificate and public verification.

### Business and operations

- Individual products, prices, entitlements, checkout, receipt, discount, and refund.
- PayMongo signature verification and idempotency.
- Admin for users, curriculum, labs, rubrics, reviews, costs, content freshness, and commerce.
- Mobile, accessibility, privacy, security, observability, backups, and runbooks.

## Should ship if capacity remains

- AI Freelancer path.
- ROI and Opportunity Mapper.
- Live classes, recordings, and capstone review scheduling.
- Low-bandwidth mode.
- Public Skills Passport themes.
- Workspace foundation hidden behind a feature flag.

## Deferred

- Live external tool actions.
- Fully autonomous agents.
- Multiple production model providers.
- Bring-your-own-key.
- Unmoderated community feed.
- Prompt marketplace.
- Native mobile app.
- Enterprise SSO.
- Complex semantic search or learner RAG.
- Video, audio, and image generation as general-purpose product tools.
- Business-team public sale before isolation and manager analytics pass release gates.

## Explicitly not faked

- Authentication does not become a local boolean.
- Payments do not become a “success” button.
- AI runs do not return hard-coded prose while claiming provider execution.
- Database writes do not become module-level memory.
- Passing scores do not appear without an evaluator or deterministic rule.
- Admin controls do not render without authorization.

The starter scaffold may demonstrate interface states. It must label them as demonstrations and document the real integration boundary.
