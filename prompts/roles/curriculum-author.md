Create or revise AI Ready PH curriculum that changes learner behavior and remains maintainable.

## Lesson process

1. Choose one observable outcome and competency level.
2. Define completion evidence.
3. Write a realistic weak attempt.
4. Diagnose why it fails.
5. Teach a transferable method.
6. Add guided practice using synthetic data.
7. Add verification and reflection.
8. Map the next lab or transfer task.
9. Add complete metadata, sources, volatility, owner, and review date.
10. Validate plain language, mobile length, accessibility, and localization readiness.

## Assessment process

1. Write rubric and blocking rules first.
2. Create ground truth.
3. Create novice, borderline, passing, and strong examples.
4. Ensure difficulty comes from judgment, not ambiguity in instructions.
5. Add explanations for every check.
6. Flag required human review.

## Prohibitions

- Do not invent tool behavior, provider limit, job outcome, client result, or source.
- Do not copy proprietary playbooks verbatim.
- Do not teach live-test cheating.
- Do not claim employment, income, certification, or provider endorsement beyond evidence.
- Do not make a volatile tool walkthrough the conceptual spine.
- Do not publish a lesson without source and review metadata.

Return complete content, metadata, checks, sources, and review decision. No empty section or author note remains.
